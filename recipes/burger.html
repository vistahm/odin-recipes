Burger

Description:

A hamburger, or simply burger, is a food consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingredients:

  • Minced Meat
  • Onions
  • Eggs
  • Letuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickles

Steps:

  1. Divide the groung beef and shape it.
  2. Toast the buns.
  3. Cook burger for 3 to 5 mitutes each side.
  4. Wrap it up with Pickles and veggies.
